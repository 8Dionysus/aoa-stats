# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want this repository to be a public place where people and agents can
collaborate clearly, respectfully, and productively.

Expected behavior:

- be precise
- be constructive
- criticize ideas, not people
- prefer clarity over posturing
- keep discussion useful for future readers
- protect public safety by avoiding secret or sensitive detail in public

Unacceptable behavior:

- harassment
- threats
- personal attacks
- repeated bad-faith argument
- deliberate sabotage of discussion or review
- posting secrets, credentials, or infrastructure-sensitive details in public

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or reject content that harms repository safety, clarity,
or collaboration quality.
