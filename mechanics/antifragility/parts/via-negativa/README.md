# via-negativa

Guides review of keep, merge, move, suppression, quarantine, deprecation, and
deletion pressure so weak signals do not become authority.

The operation payload is the human review checklist in
`docs/VIA_NEGATIVA_CHECKLIST.md`. Its bounded stats question and owner-return
posture are authored in
`stats/operation-contracts/active/antifragility.via-negativa.operation.json`.
The focused tests prove that the checklist preserves common-center order and
stop lines while the source record remains checklist-only and
`symbolic_unbound`. They do not prove that a review occurred. No automatic
deletion or mutation is implemented by this part.
