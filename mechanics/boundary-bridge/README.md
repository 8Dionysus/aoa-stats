# Boundary bridge

This package maps the common AoA Boundary Bridge mechanic into `aoa-stats`.

## Mechanic card

Status: mapped common-center mechanic with three active part routes. Payloads
are localized or retained as explicit public routes in `mechanics/topology.json`.

### Trigger

Use this package when source-owned facts must enter the shared stats receipt
envelope, or when a derived stats catalog must tell a consumer where stronger
owner truth lives and how cautiously to read the projection.

### Local owns

`aoa-stats` owns the shared receipt envelope, event-kind admission registry,
derived surface profiles, intake-coverage signals, and their stats-side
validation.

### Stronger owner split

Source repositories own payload meaning and local truth. `Agents-of-Abyss`
owns the common Boundary Bridge mechanic. Consumer repositories own their own
policy and actions. `aoa-evals` keeps proof authority.

### Inputs

Source-owned receipt payloads and owner references; stats surface definitions,
coverage results, and authority ceilings.

### Outputs

An admitted shared envelope plus descriptive catalog and coverage signals that
send consumers back to stronger owner sources when needed.

### Must not claim

Payload ownership, proof verdict, owner health, route approval, workflow
activation, or permission to mutate a source system.
