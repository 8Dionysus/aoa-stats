# Recurrence derived summaries for stats

`aoa-stats` may consume recurrence projections as derived observability.

Allowed generated candidates:

- `generated/recurrence_coverage_summary.min.json`
- `generated/recurrence_pressure_summary.min.json`

These surfaces summarize counts and movement across recurrence artifacts. They must not decide whether a technique is canonical, whether a skill should activate, whether an eval is portable proof, or whether a playbook owns a scenario.
