# Contract

- Inputs remain owned by the repositories named in the read-model profile.
- aoa-stats may derive and publish bounded observations only.
- The result never becomes proof, routing, gate, identity, or workflow truth.
- Every part-local or retained public payload route is declared in
  `mechanics/topology.json` and linked from `stats/read-models/`.
- The part-local validator owns the build, verify, registry, fail-closed
  pre-materialization, materialization, and trust-gate roundtrip.
- `scripts/validate_abyss_machine_summary_catalog_bundle.py` remains a thin
  compatibility command and does not own a second validator implementation.
