# Contract

## Input

Owner-local adoption and recurrence signals with explicit evidence, retention,
and review posture.

## Output

Derived dashboards, rollups, and metric-event contracts that preserve the
source owner and promotion gates.

## Boundary

No direct KAG or Tree of Sophia promotion, no owner consent inference, no
proof-by-volume, and no hidden adoption action.

## Crosswalk

This non-catalog observation contract cross-routes to
`stats/operation-contracts/`; the reciprocal route is enforced by
`mechanics/topology.json`.
