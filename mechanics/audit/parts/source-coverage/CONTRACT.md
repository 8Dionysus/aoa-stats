# Contract

- Inputs remain owned by the repositories named in the read-model profile.
- aoa-stats may derive and publish bounded observations only.
- The result never becomes proof, routing, gate, identity, or workflow truth.
- Every part-local or retained public payload route is declared in
  `mechanics/topology.json` and linked from `stats/read-models/`.
- Receipt and registry ordering cannot change the projection.
- Owner and event-kind counts conserve the admitted, owner-addressable input;
  invalid or missing owner identity is not promoted into an owner bucket.
- Missing registry context remains explicit and cannot manufacture expected,
  missing, or unexpected owners.
- Thin flags are descriptive audit signals. They never become owner-health,
  proof, routing, scoring, or activation fields.
